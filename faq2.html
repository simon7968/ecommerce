Orders & Delivery

We offer complimentary white-glove delivery across India. Standard delivery takes 7–10 business days. Express delivery (3–5 days) is available for select pin codes at an additional charge. You will receive real-time tracking once your order is dispatched.

Yes. Once your order is confirmed and dispatched, you will receive an email with a tracking link. You can also view your order status anytime from your En'Decore account dashboard.

Currently, we ship across all major Indian cities. International shipping is available to select countries — please contact our client services team for a personalised shipping quote.

Returns & Warranty

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, simply contact our client services team and we will arrange a complimentary pickup and full refund. Items must be in their original condition.

Every En'Decore piece carries a 5-year structural warranty covering manufacturing defects and frame integrity. Upholstery and surface finishes are covered for 2 years against manufacturing faults.

Products & Customisation

Absolutely. En'Decore offers bespoke customisation on most pieces including fabric selection, finish, and dimensions. Our interior design consultants can guide you through options — contact us or book a free consultation via our Contact page.

Our Augmented Reality feature lets you place a true-to-scale 3D model of any piece in your space using your smartphone. Simply tap "Try in AR" on a product page and follow the on-screen prompts — no app download required for most devices.

Yes. We are committed to responsible sourcing. Our hardwoods are FSC-certified, and we work exclusively with suppliers who meet our environmental and social standards. Our upholstery materials are OEKO-TEX certified and free from harmful substances.

Payments

We accept all major credit and debit cards, UPI, Net Banking, and EMI options (0% on select cards for 6–24 months). All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.
